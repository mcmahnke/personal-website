About Me

While I don't yet have any professional experience, I'll be completing an internship in software engineering this summer with a focus in working with Python. I have a lot of areas of computer science I'm interested in that I've yet to experience fully, which makes it difficult to visualize what my future career may be like, but I'm very interested in machine learning and full-stack development!
